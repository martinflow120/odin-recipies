lasagna

This is the best lasagna in the world made with wagyu beef pork fat and the best pasta and tomato sauce you can find in the market

  • pasta
  • wagyu beef
  • tomato sauce
  • parmesan cheese
    Steps
  1. buy all the ingredients
  2. boil some water
  3. put the pasta in the water for 15min
  4. prepare the tomato sauce
  5. chop the tomatoes and mix them with some oil and salt and cook them till thick
  6. prepare the wagyu beef in a pan with garlic fresh basel and butter until the meet is medium rare
  7. chop the beef in little slices
  8. in a bowl put a layer of pasta then tomato sauce then beef and repeat until you run out of ingredients

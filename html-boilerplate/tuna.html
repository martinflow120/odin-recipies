tuna

This tuna is the freshets tuna in the world bought in the fish market every morning so the costumer can enjoy the best tuna

    ingredients
  • tuna
  • soy sauce
  • butter
  • salt
  • pepper
    Steps
  1. with paper dry the tuna
  2. season the tuna with salt an pepper
  3. in a pan cook the tuna in high heat
  4. when de tuna is seared put the butter and the soy sauce
  5. when the tuna is mixed with the sauce serve an enjoy
